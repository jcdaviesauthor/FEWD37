History of the Emoji

The Millennial Generation believes that Emojis are a relatively new invention, belonging only to them, but in fact recently surfaced evidence shows that the Emojis date back to Babylonian and even Egyptian times. See early evidence of the Emojis existence below:

But even after centuries of use the true meaning of most Emojis continues to be illusive. Leading to misinterpretations and even altercations. Emoji Libs, created by designer, developer, and wah-nah-bee anthropologist, works to solve these riddles. Emoji Libs gives you real examples so you can practice how to use Emojis properly improving your Emoji intelligence quotient (or EIQ) dramatically overtime.
