History of The Emoji

The Millennial generation believes that Emojis are a relatively new invention and belong only to them, but in fact recently surfaced evidence shows that the Emojis date back to Babylonian and even Egyptian times. See early evidence of the Emojis existence below.

But even after centuries of use the true meaning of most Emojis continues to be illusive. Leading to misinterpretations and even altercations. Emoji Libs, created by designer, developer, and wah-nah-bee anthropologist Jessica Davies, works to solve these riddles.
